About Us:



Who We Are and Where We Are Going:

Welcome to Taco Pariosa, where the vibrant flavors of authentic Mexican cuisine meet the warmth of a cozy, welcoming tropical atmosphere. The first Taco Paraiso location was opened in 2018 by Christian Cook in Marco Island, Florida and it soon became a massive hit for vacation goers.

Popular with the vacation crowd, the restaurant soon evolved into a chain and opened an additional restaurant in Flordia and another two locations in Hawaii. Due to continuing success, Taco Paraiso is expanding to another location in Pensacola Beach, Flordia and will open in the early years of 2025.

The already-lengthy menu has been changed and expanded over the years, and several items have grown in popularity. We also offer great seasonal items based on the time of year. We like to thank those customers who allowed us to be here where we are today and we will stop at nothing to serve you the paradise you promptly deserve.
